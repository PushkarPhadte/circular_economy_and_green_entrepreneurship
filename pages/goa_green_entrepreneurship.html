Goa: A Hub for Green Entrepreneurship

Discover sustainable businesses and visionary changemakers transforming Goa's economy.

Why Goa is Perfect for Green Entrepreneurship

Goa's unique combination of environmental awareness, tourism-driven economy, and progressive policies creates an ideal ecosystem for green entrepreneurs. The state's compact size and engaged community make it a perfect testing ground for sustainable innovations.

🌊

Rich Biodiversity

Coastal-urban challenges drive innovation in marine conservation, waste management, and eco-tourism.

📜

Policy Support

India's first comprehensive Resource Efficiency & Circular Economy Strategy provides strong framework.

👥

Engaged Community

High environmental awareness among residents and tourists creates ready market for green solutions.

💼

Business Ecosystem

Vibrant market for green hospitality, renewable energy, sustainable tourism, and clean mobility.

Leading Green Entrepreneurs in Goa

These innovative companies are pioneering sustainable solutions across mobility, food, tourism, energy, and waste management sectors.

Sustainable Mobility

🏍️

Kabira Mobility

Electric Two-Wheeler Manufacturing

Goa-based manufacturer of high-performance e-bikes like the KM5000, India's fastest electric bike with a range up to 344 km per charge. Driving innovation in clean urban mobility with advanced battery technology and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Clean Transport Innovation
🚲

BLive

EV Tourism & Fleet Management

India's first EV tourism platform offering e-bike experiences and electric fleet management for businesses and tourists. Making green mobility accessible and convenient across Goa's popular destinations with smart charging infrastructure.

Tourism E-Mobility

Sustainable Food & Agriculture

🌱

Wakao Foods

Plant-Based Food Innovation

Pioneering sustainable food by producing healthy, ready-to-cook jackfruit products. Promotes plant-based diets, sources locally to minimize environmental footprint, and supports rural farmers through direct procurement.

Plant-Based Local Sourcing

Clean Energy & Technology

❄️

SMDPower Solutions

Energy-Efficient Cooling

Provides IoT-powered, energy-saving cooling solutions reducing power consumption by up to 40% for air conditioning in homes and commercial spaces. Smart monitoring and automated controls minimize emissions across Goa.

IoT Energy Efficiency

Waste Reduction & Circular Economy

💧

Carry Your Bottle

Plastic-Free Movement

Leading Goa's sustainability movement by encouraging reusable bottles and operating refill stations across the state. Fighting single-use plastic waste through community engagement and public-private partnerships.

Zero Waste Community

Startup Ecosystem Support

🚀

FiiRE Goa

Sustainability Incubator

Goa's premier incubator accelerating climate, circular economy, and tourism startups with funding, mentorship, and industry partnerships. Connects entrepreneurs with investors, corporates, and government programs.

Incubation Funding

Success Stories

From Beach Pollution to Business Opportunity

2022

A group of Goan youth transformed beach waste collection into a thriving eco-enterprise, processing 2 tonnes of plastic weekly and employing 15 local workers through partnership with Carry Your Bottle.

Electric Tourism Fleet Expansion

2023

BLive's e-bike tourism service expanded from 50 to 300 vehicles across Goa, offering 100,000+ green rides to tourists and reducing carbon emissions by 450 tonnes annually.

Kabira's Export Achievement

2024

Kabira Mobility began exporting electric bikes to Southeast Asian markets, establishing Goa as a hub for high-performance EV manufacturing with plans to produce 50,000 units annually.

Government Policies & Support Programs

♻️

Goa Waste Management Corporation

State-wide green waste reforms, recycling hubs, and Extended Producer Responsibility implementation supporting waste-to-wealth startups.

  • 10+ Material Recovery Facilities
  • Daily processing of 7.5 tonnes dry waste
  • Support for waste collection enterprises
☀️

Solar City Goa Initiative

Comprehensive solar energy program with subsidies, e-mobility pilots, and energy audits for SMEs promoting clean energy entrepreneurship.

  • 30% subsidy on rooftop solar
  • Net metering for surplus power
  • Green building certification support
🏖️

Eco-Tourism Circuits

Sustainable tourism development through turtle-tracking beaches, river cleanups, and homestay partnerships creating opportunities for eco-tourism entrepreneurs.

  • Certification for eco-friendly hotels
  • Beach cleanup employment programs
  • Wildlife tourism guidelines
🎓

Circular Goa Fellowship

Grants for student-led circular innovation projects across the state, fostering next generation of green entrepreneurs.

  • ₹5 lakh per project funding
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Demo day with investors

Funding & Resources for Green Entrepreneurs

Startup India Seed Fund

Up to ₹50 lakh for proof of concept and prototype development for green startups.

BIRAC BioNEST

Bio-incubation support for sustainable agriculture and food processing ventures.

FiiRE Accelerator

3-month program with ₹10 lakh funding, mentorship, and market access for climate startups.

Angel Investment Network Goa

Connect with local and national investors interested in sustainable businesses.

MSME Green Credit Scheme

Low-interest loans up to ₹1 crore for eco-friendly manufacturing and services.

Goa Technology Hub

Co-working space, prototyping facilities, and networking events for tech entrepreneurs.
